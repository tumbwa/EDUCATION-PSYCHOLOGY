CHILD COGNITIVE DEVELOPMENT

Cognitive Psychology- studies mental processes including how people think, perceive, remember and learn. focusing on a child's development in terms of information processing, conceptual resources, perceptual skill, language learning, and other aspects of the development. Some refer our brain as computer, as it takes input, process information, and store and retrieve it. Human life and biological support system are governed directed and coordinated by the brain. Brain carries its functions through a constant exchange of message (mental process).

The mental process of human being which include how people know, understand, process information, make judgment and decisions, use of language and understand is called cognition. Cognition depend on parent’s genes/ inheritance and interaction with environment . Increasing of cognition ability and change is known as cognitive development

Cognitive development- means the increase development of a children mental ability in reasoning, problem solving recalling, language perception, understanding, logic etc.

A human baby is born with a few practical instincts and reflexes such as sucking and grasping. The initial infant cognitive structure suppose to incorporate only to those cognitive abilities and / potentials which help them to do such acts. Swiss psychologist and biologist Jean Piaget term this cognitive potential ability as schemata, singular schema. Schema therefore represents a unity of one’s cognitive structure in shape of general potential to perform a particular class of behaviors like sucking.

The various schemas with their contents thus form a basic human mind structure. The areas schemas represent those reflexes and instincts that are biologically inherited. Due to physical and social environment child form different schemata led to modification of cognitive structure as he/ she adapt.

FACTORS AFFECTING COGNITIVE DEVELOPMENT

According to Piaget, there is a relationship between intelligence and heredity, to him intelligence is the product of hereditary factor and environment but neither alone determines intelligence. Environmental factor for intelligence include social, physical, economic up brings, health care, nutrition, stability of family and states etc.

PIAGET COGNITIVE DEVELOPMENT THEORY

To Piaget, cognitive development was a progressive reorganization of mental processes resulting from biological maturation and environmental experience. He believed that children construct an understanding of the world around them, experience discrepancies between what they already know and what they discover in their environment, then adjust their ideas accordingly. According to jean piaget all child over the world develop cognitively in sequence.
