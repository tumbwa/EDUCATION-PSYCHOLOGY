CHILD DEVELOPMENT

Developmental psychology deals with how people grow and change over the course of lifetime. developmental psychology not only concerned with the physical changes that occur as people grow; they also look at the social, emotional, and cognitive development that occurs throughout life.

Generally

Is a biological and psychological change that occurs in human being between conception and the end of adolescence to death as individual progress from dependably to autonomy

Development- refers to long-term personal changes that have multiple source and multiple effects. It is a lifelong beginning before birth and extending to death. physical changes largely drived the process, as our cognitive abilities advanced and declined function in old age. Our development depend on heredity and environments, our behavior and ability at any stage in life, depend on continuously interaction with them. For example all human infants are born with ability to learn language, this is only for human , other organism does not have this ability. This is something that are biological inherited.
Generally our development is affected by two factors, which are heredity and environments. For example ability to speak particular language at particular stage is result of maturation, while speaking language depend on culture (which will determine types of language you speak) is a result of learning in order to gain or acquire that language. Development depend on the sequence , usually simple to complex actions or events. For example infant will start sitting before crawling before running. There are various defined period in a child development, this is exampleof age related development periods :
• New born (age 0-4 weeks)
• Infant (age 4 weeks – 1 year)
• Toddler (age 1 year -3 years)
• Preschool (age 3 year -6 year)
• School age (6 -11 years )
• Adolescent ( 11 -18 years )

Our biological predisposition in which we are born with which we are born with are expressed itself through the process of maturation. Maturation refer to the inborn(innate) determined sequence of growth or body changes that are relatively independent from training /experience. STAGES OF HUMAN DEVELOPMENT

we can categorize stage of human development into two stage. first stage is pre-natal and second stage is post-natal. natal is of or related to birth. so simply this stages are before birth and second stages is after. Pre –natal development; Period from conception to birth it is process by which in nine month a single cell develops into highly complex human being over 800 billion cells. This stage is divided into three stages which are:

I. Germinal period: Almost immediately after fertilization zygote the process of cell division that will eventually produce a human body made up of billion of cells.

II. The Embryonic period: Within 4 weeks after conception the organism is already about long e.g. heart is beating to pump blood through microscopic veins and arteries, the beginning of brain, kidney, livers and digestive track appeared. Indentations at the top will eventually become jaws, eyes and ears.

III. The Fetal period : About eight weeks after Conceptions when the major organs have taken shape, here development shift from rudimentary (body part with no function) to organism structure and establish their functions. Now is ready to start life as a fully developed infant.
