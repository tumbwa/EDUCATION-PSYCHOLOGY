EDUCATIONAL PSYCHOLOGY

Is the branch of psychology in which the finding of psychology is applied in field of education –it is scientific study of human behavior in educational settings. It concern with understanding how Instructional environment and the characteristics of learner interact to procedure cognate growth in learner . Is scientific discipline that is concerned with understanding and improving how students acquire variety capabilities through formed instruction in classroom setting. Educational Psychology - emphasize that teachers and students use psychological approaches to understand, predict and control learning opportunity that schooling offers
1. Omar (2006)defines Educational Psychology as the application psychology and psychological methods to the study of human development, learning, motivation, instruction , assessment and related issues that influence and that interaction of teaching and learning .
2. Crow and crow ( 1973) argue that educational psychology describe and explain the learning experiences of an individual from birth to through old stage.
To Skinner (1958) education psychology is the branch of psychology that deals with teaching and learning. Philosopher Johann Herbart as the "father" of educational psychology. Herbart believed that a student's interest ( by intrinsic or extrinsic motivated) in a topic had a tremendous influence on the learning outcome and believed that teachers should consider this interest along with prior knowledge when deciding which type of instruction is most appropriate.

Functions of eductional psychology


1. To give through knowledge of the nature of the child.
2. To provide significant knowledge of developmental process with particular emphasis upon the promotion guidance and control of mental aspects of learner.
3. To provide an understanding of the principle governing together with a knowledge and application to particular problems in the classroom
4. To present the theories underlying the measurement and evaluation of the mental abilities , aptitudes , achievements , interest and personality organization
5. Help to understand the process of teaching and learning that takes place within formal environment and developing ways of improving those methods.
IMPLICATION
1-It consider how the educational process affects students understanding better teaching- learning techniques and understanding teachers – student interaction
2- It supplies necessary knowledge and skills especially for the teacher to realize the objectives of education for essential scientific skills, teaching expertise and advice in mounding and shaping the behavior of his students.
